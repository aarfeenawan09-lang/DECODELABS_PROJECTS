About GameHub

Built by gamers, for gamers.

Our Story

GameHub started in a small dorm room with one big idea: gear shopping for gamers shouldn't feel like a chore. What began as a side project comparing controller prices has grown into a full storefront for consoles, PCs, and accessories — built by people who actually play the games they sell.

Our Mission

We believe great gear shouldn't come with a guessing game. Every product on GameHub is tested, every price is fair, and every order ships fast — so you can spend less time shopping and more time playing.
